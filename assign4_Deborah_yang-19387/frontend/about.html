Welcome to Univerity of Houston Sugarland Cafeteria! This site was made to help students learn more about our new dining hall that we are excited to talk about. We opened Ellis Dining Hall to help the growing student at UH Sugarland.

Learn More About our Director

Deborah Yang is the Director of Ellis Dining. She has been with UH for three years and was passionate about helping the UH Sugarland student body grow and encourage a more lively community to grow. Apart of the mission that Ms. Yang has set out on was making sure that all communities will have options and be able to enjoy Ellis Dining Hall. She graduated with a Food Management Major and has spent her career honing her craft to ensure to help all types of student bodies grow and connect.
